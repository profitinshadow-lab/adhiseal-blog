Large Format Tiles — Pro Tips

Aaj kal 600x1200mm aur 800x800mm tiles ka trend hai. Ye tiles luxurious dikhti hain lekin inhe lagana ek skill hai. Galat method se tiles crack ho sakti hain ya uneven finish aa sakta hai.

Kyun Alag Hoti Hain Large Format Tiles?

Weight

600x1200mm granite tile ka weight 15-20kg tak ho sakta hai. Is weight ko support karne ke liye high-strength adhesive zaroori hai.

Warping Tendency

Badi tiles ka surface perfectly flat nahi hota — inka natural warping hota hai. Adhesive mein flexibility honi chahiye.

Coverage Requirement

Choti tiles mein 60-70% coverage kaafi hoti hai. Large tiles mein minimum 95% back coverage zaroori hai.

AdhiSeal Elite — Large Format Ke Liye Best

AdhiSeal Elite specifically large format tiles ke liye design kiya gaya hai:

  • High flexibility — polymer-modified formula
  • Non-slump — vertical/ceiling applications mein bhi tile slide nahi hoti
  • Extended open time — 45-60 minutes tak work kar sakte hain

Step-by-Step Method

Step 1: Surface Preparation

Substrate perfectly level hona chahiye. 3m se zyada lambi surface mein 3mm se zyada variation allowed nahi.

Step 2: Mixing

AdhiSeal Elite ko clean water mein mix karein (0.45-0.50 water/powder ratio). 5 minute rest ke baad ek baar aur mix karein.

Step 3: Double-Buttering

Both substrate aur tile ke peeche adhesive lagaen — ye technique 95%+ coverage ensure karti hai.

Step 4: Notched Trowel

10mm x 10mm ya 12mm x 12mm square notch trowel use karein.

Step 5: Beating

Mallet se tile ko firmly press karein — complete contact ke liye.

Common Mistakes

  • ❌ Thin adhesive bed
  • ❌ Open time exceed karna
  • ❌ Insufficient back-buttering
  • ✅ Always use lippage control clips
