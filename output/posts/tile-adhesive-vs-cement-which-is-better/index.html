Tile Adhesive vs Cement — Kya Fark Hai?

Purane zamane mein har koi cement-sand ka mixture use karta tha tiles lagane ke liye. Lekin aaj ke modern construction mein tile adhesive ek superior choice ban gaya hai. Chaliye detail mein samjhte hain.

Cement-Sand Method Ki Problems

  • Uneven surface: Cement mix manually prepare karna hota hai — consistency maintain karna mushkil hota hai
  • Shrinkage cracking: Cement set hone ke baad shrink karta hai, jis se tiles crack ho sakti hain
  • Low bond strength: Wet areas mein (bathroom, kitchen) cement ka bond kamzor pad jaata hai
  • More material use: Thicker layer laani padti hai — zyada weight aur cost

Tile Adhesive Ke Fayde

Strong Bonding

AdhiSeal ke polymer-modified tile adhesives ek molecular-level bond banate hain jo cement se kaafi zyada strong hota hai.

Water Resistance

Bathroom, kitchen, swimming pools — AdhiSeal adhesive wet conditions mein bhi apni grip maintain karta hai.

Thin Bed Application

Sirf 3-4mm ki layer kaafi hai — cement ke comparison mein kaafi kam material use hota hai.

Flexibility

AdhiSeal Super aur Elite grades mein polymer additives hote hain jo minor movement absorb kar lete hain — tiles pop nahi hoti.

Kab Cement Use Karein?

Bahut thick surfaces level karne ke liye ya screed banane ke liye cement use karna theek hai. Lekin final tile bonding ke liye hamesha tile adhesive hi use karein.

Conclusion

Agar aap ek baar tile lagane ke baad dhang se bhoolna chahte hain, to AdhiSeal tile adhesive hi best investment hai. Lamba time, strong grip, aur professional finish.
