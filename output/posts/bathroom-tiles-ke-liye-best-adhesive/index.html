Bathroom Mein Tile Adhesive Kyun Zaroori Hai?

Bathroom ek wet area hai — hamesha moisture present rehti hai. Agar sahi adhesive nahi use kiya to:

  • Tiles peel off ho jaati hain
  • Grout crumble ho jaata hai
  • Walls mein seepage shuru ho jaati hai

AdhiSeal Premium — Bathroom Ka Safest Choice

AdhiSeal Premium adhesive bathroom walls aur floors dono ke liye suitable hai. Iska water-resistant formula ensure karta hai ki tiles daani tak gripped rahein.

Floor vs Wall Adhesive — Kya Fark?

Floor

Floor par weight aata hai is mehnat mein non-slip aur high-compressive-strength adhesive chahiye. AdhiSeal Standard+ ya Super use karein.

Wall

Wall tiles light hoti hain lekin gravitational pull fight karna padta hai. AdhiSeal Premium (non-slump) ideal hai.

Tips For Bathroom Tiles

1. Epoxy grout use karein — regular grout absorbs moisture

2. Waterproofing membrane pehle lagaen wet areas mein

3. Minimum 6mm movement joints rakhein around perimeter

4. 24 hours wait karne ke baad grout karein
