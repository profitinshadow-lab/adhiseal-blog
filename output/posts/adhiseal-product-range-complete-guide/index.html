AdhiSeal Ki 4 Product Grades

AdhiSeal ke paas 4 tile adhesive grades hain — har ek alag application ke liye designed hai.

AdhiSeal Premium — Entry Level, Premium Quality

Best For: Interior walls aur floors, ceramic aur standard vitrified tiles

Coverage: ~4-5 sq.m per bag

Tile Size: Up to 400x400mm

Ghar ke normal renovation projects ke liye yeh sab se popular choice hai. Reliable bonding, easy mixing, aur affordable price.

AdhiSeal Standard+ — Everyday Professional Choice

Best For: Contractors ke daily projects, ceramic aur vitrified tiles

Coverage: ~4.5-5.5 sq.m per bag

Tile Size: Up to 600x600mm

Smooth application aur consistent performance ke liye contractors Standard+ prefer karte hain.

AdhiSeal Super — High-Performance Formula

Best For: High-movement areas, exterior tiles, heavy traffic floors

Coverage: ~4-5 sq.m per bag

Tile Size: Up to 600x600mm

Polished tiles, glass mosaic, aur tough site conditions ke liye Super ka high-flexibility formula perfect hai.

AdhiSeal Elite — Heavy Duty, Large Format

Best For: Large format tiles (600x600mm se bade), swimming pools, commercial projects

Coverage: ~3.5-4.5 sq.m per bag

Tile Size: 600x600mm se 1200x2400mm tak

Sabse strong formula — non-slump, extended open time, maximum flexibility.
