Tile Adhesive Mein Paani Kitna Daalen? — Mixing Ka Sahi Ratio

✍️ AdhiSeal Team
📅 1 March 2026
⏱ 3 minute read
🧱

Mixing Ratio — Ek Galatfehmi

Bahut log tile adhesive mein andaaze se paani daalte hain — yahi sabse badi galati hai. Mixing ratio directly bond strength aur workability affect karta hai.

AdhiSeal Products Ka Standard Ratio

ProductPowderWater
AdhiSeal Premium20 kg4.8-5.2 litre
AdhiSeal Standard+20 kg4.5-5.0 litre
AdhiSeal Super20 kg4.5-5.0 litre
AdhiSeal Elite20 kg4.2-4.6 litre

Sahi Consistency Test

AdhiSeal mix kar ke trowel se uthao — agar bata khud ruk jaaye aur dharam se na giray, consistency sahi hai.

Zyada Paani — Kya Hota Hai?

  • Bond strength kam hoti hai
  • Shrinkage cracking zyada
  • Sagging (vertical applications mein slide)

Kam Paani — Kya Hota Hai?

  • Apply karna mushkil
  • Coverage inconsistent
  • Proper wetting nahi hoti

Pro Tip

Pehle paani daalo, baad mein powder milao — iski taraf dhyan do ulta nahi. Aur ek baar mix karne ke baad 3-5 minute wait karo fir dobara mix karo.
